Warehousing and Transloading

Warehousing is “Receiving” “Identifying” “Holding” and “Packaging” hence preparing them for delivery.

Warehousing and distribution are an integrated part of CityLinx. We Logistics. We provide warehousing as a component of distribution to optimize and streamline our client’s warehousing and distribution processes.

Our offerings include:

➤ Container storage

➤ Pallet storage (block/racked stacking)

➤ Container packing/unpacking

➤ Labelling

➤ Palletizing

➤ Stretch/Shrinkwrapping

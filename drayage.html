Drayage

Container movement within all 48 States.

We provide Container drayage shipping which is the process of moving container freight within the State or interstate between ports, facilities, rail yards, or other shipping hubs. Drayage transportation is handled by trucks.

The service is in extremely high demand in large port cities, like Los Angeles, Miami, New York, Baltimore, Seattle, and more.

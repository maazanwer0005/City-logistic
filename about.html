About Us

City Logistics is focused on exceeding our customers' expectations by providing the best drayage services that are responsibly priced, timely, efficient, damage free, and in conformity with the highest industry standards.

Our dispatchers understand the time-sensitive nature of the goods; as a result, we have an excellent track record. The exceptional service to our customers is leading to our continued growth and success.

We at City Logistics recognize that each client is unique, therefore, their requirements for shipping and handling are unique. At our core, we are problem solvers, and we'll take the time to custom-design a freight solution to meet all your needs. We improve profitability for customers by allowing them to focus on their core competencies while we manage their logistics, providing truckload and container drayage.

Unlike other brokerage companies, we can offer an array of services that will full fill all of your cargo needs. Our commitment to priority service will ensure that your shipments are handled professionally and promptly. We aim to exceed our client's expectations by providing exceptional services, strategic solutions, and unmatched client satisfaction.
